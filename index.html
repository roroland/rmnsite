I'm a self-taught web developer and musician, based in Argentina, working fully remote. I've started learning about web development around the year 2000 helping my brother to build a news website about latin rock. After that I focus in learning Flash development (it was the hype back then).

Soon I get my firsts jobs building small html & flash web sites for local stores and small companies, most of the time working remotely for clients in USA.

In 2004, along with a couple of friends in Argentina and Spain, founded a small web design studio called SonicByte.
We make together lots of small to medium-size projects for almost 8 years, ranging from local food restaurants, custom made wholesale stores, e-commerce sites upto some crazy MVP experiments of social-based web apps for the university of Zaragoza, Spain (BIFI).

After that I worked briefly developing the layout of 'Remix Learning', a cloud-base social network for primary and high school education for the Chicago University.

In 2014 I joined the team of 'Spines', a learning web app start up. And work with them for almost 4 years developing the company website, the web app, browser extension and a basic mobile app.

In 2017 I joined 'Central de Reservas' a major european online travel agency, and currently still working with them.
